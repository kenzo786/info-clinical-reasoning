[sc name="map-steps-and-links" id="1277" anchor="106" title="Red Flags" color="pink" step="6" info-modal="" pathway="display-none" youtube="display-none"][/sc]<details class="kb-accordion kb-parent d-pink" open> <summary id="s-is.106" role="button">Red flag synthesis</summary> <ul class="no-bullet"> <li><strong>Emergency same-day:</strong> suspected sepsis, hypotension, confusion, severe systemic illness, inability to keep fluids down.</li> <li><strong>Urgent same-day:</strong> fever with loin pain, vomiting, rigors, pregnancy with systemic illness, suspected pyelonephritis.</li> <li><strong>Prompt primary care review:</strong> recurrent UTIs, treatment failure, atypical symptoms, or persistent haematuria.</li> <li><strong>Alternative pathway:</strong> vaginal discharge, itch, or [sc name="jitl-term-link" Q="STI assessment in primary care and sexual health" text="STI"][/sc] risk should move the patient away from a simple UTI pathway.</li> </ul> </details> [sc name="jitl-link-06" Q="Cystitis (UTI in Women)"][/sc]
